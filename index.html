Hey, I'm Julia Markou,
I'm a Ph.D. Student in the Technical University of Denmark, Department of Management Engineering. My research interests include machine learning in transportation, simulation of transportation systems, Intelligent Transport Systems (ITS), calibration and optimization applications.
